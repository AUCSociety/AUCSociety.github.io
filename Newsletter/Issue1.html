AUCS focuses on 3 core areas, social, informative, and competition. We push beyond what's available at the University, giving tech enthusiastic students a wide range of opportunities to collaborate with others, compete and showcase their talents as well as having a lot of fun.

Kicking off this semester with some great moments, let's take a quick look back at the first quarter of 2017!

Congratulations to the first and second place teams of the AUCS Welcoming Night beer pong tournament. Winning prizes of $50 and $25 Shadow's bar tabs, with their sharp aim, and passion for beer.
